The health tracker keeps a rolling window of the last 10 jobs. Below 30% success rate means the operator drops out of rotation. A 60-second cooldown kicks in after every failure too. Fail every other job and half your uptime is spent locked out.

Quote lifetime is the tuning surface. Default is 5 minutes, protocol max is 1 hour. Too short and users can't complete the flow before expiry. Too long and the operator is locked into stale pricing. Finding the right window depends on how volatile the underlying asset prices are.

Before slashing there are four recovery stages: heartbeat drift, degraded status, offline, then slashing risk. The dispute window is 3.5 days. Every stage before slashing is recoverable with a single successful heartbeat. The system gives operators every chance to come back.
